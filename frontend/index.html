Frequently asked

Does a bot apply for me?

No. The AI only finds and scores jobs. A trained human applies to each one by hand.

Why do you ask for email access?

Read-only access lets us track recruiter replies — how many responded, your response ratio and interview invites. We never send email as you.

What if I don't have time to approve?

No problem. If you can't review in time, our admin team can approve your top-scored matches so applying never stalls.

Can I download my reports?

Yes — filter by company, date or match score, then export a clean PDF report any time.
